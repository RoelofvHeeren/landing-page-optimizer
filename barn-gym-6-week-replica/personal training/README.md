# Welcome Offer & Local Training Optimization

This folder documents the strategic shift for the Etchingham location landing page.

## Objectives
- **Local Intent Capture**: Specifically target "gym near me" and "gym etchingham" traffic.
- **Immediate Value**: Position the 6-Week Transformation as the "Welcome Offer" to reduce entry friction.
- **Direct Engagement**: Replace the VSL with a high-converting lead form for immediate information requests.

## Strategy
People searching for a "gym in Etchingham" might be confused by a standalone "6-week transformation" pitch. By branding it as the **Welcome Offer**, we acknowledge their primary search (a gym) while immediately presenting the best way to get started.
